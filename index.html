Day 1(am): FOUNDEVER

Foundever is a top-tier global CX provider with strong scale, advanced tech, and a socially-driven mission. It offers solid benefits and entry-level opportunities, especially in the Philippines. They showcase their training facilities where new hires are equipped with essential skills, followed by a guided tour of the operations floor to observe how agents handle real-time customer interactions using CRM tools and performance monitoring systems. We learned about the innovative technologies they use, such as AI-driven chatbots and data analytics, and how they prioritize employee well-being and a positive workplace culture.

Day 1(pm): GOOGLE PHILIPPINES

Day 2(am): TELEPERFORMANCE

Teleperformance is a global leader in outsourced omnichannel customer experience management. The visit featured a tour of their operations, a discussion on BPO industry trends, and a Q&A with their management team.

Day 2(pm): KOLLAB

Kollab is a collaborative workspace and tech incubator. The visit included a tour of their co-working spaces, a talk on startup culture, and networking with resident entrepreneurs.

Day 3(am): ASIAN DEVELOPMENT BANK

Asian Development Bank (ADB) is a regional development bank. The visit provided insights into their IT infrastructure, digital transformation projects, and career opportunities in international development.

Day 3(pm): MICROSOURCING

Microsourcing is a leading provider of outsourcing solutions. The tour included a walkthrough of their facilities, a session on digital careers, and a panel with their HR and operations teams.
